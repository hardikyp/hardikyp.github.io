About Me

I am a Ph.D. Candidate in Civil Engineering and Scientific Computing at the University of Michigan. I hold M.S.E. in Civil (Structures) Engineering from the University of Michigan and B. Tech. (with honors) in Civil Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay, India. My current research is focused on employing the concepts of curved-crease origami to generate smooth surfaces with shape morphing capabilities and tunable performance characteristics around the fluid flow.
