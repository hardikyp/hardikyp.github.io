Deployable Bridges Paper Accepted to ASCE EMI 2025

Our manuscript, “Topology Optimisation for Adaptive Bridge Systems,” has been accepted for presentation at the ASCE Engineering Mechanics Institute conference in Seattle. The paper captures our latest simulation results and a new workflow that couples origami-based kinematics with nonlinear structural analysis.

Between now and the conference we will be polishing the open-source toolchain and preparing a demonstration model that showcases rapid deployment under load. If you plan to attend EMI, let me know—I would love to compare notes.
