Milestone Nov 5, 2024

Keynote at the Graduate Research Symposium

I was invited to deliver the keynote address for the 2024 U-M Civil & Environmental Engineering Graduate Research Symposium. The talk walked through our approach to designing shape-morphing structural systems, including lessons from the Michigan Mars Habitat project.

Sharing the stage with colleagues across structures, materials, and transportation reminded me how interdisciplinary this work is. Slides and references are available on request, and I plan to record a shorter version for the site soon.
